DMCA Policy:

Eligibility for DMCA

For a DMCA you must be the developer of the game you want to take down and follow the requests form in the email you send.

DMCA process

After you've sent your request, you'll have to wait for us to see the request. For faster replies you can tag us on Discord. We are going to check your request, negotiate and if you don't agree we'll just take the game down as requested.

How to request a DMCA

Send us an email at support@itsaleak.xyz and please specify the name of your game, takedown reason, if negotiable and if you tried contacting us before.
